Our Curriculum

Our daily schedule is packed with engaging activities tailored to different age groups. We focus on key developmental areas:

  • Literacy Fun: Storytelling, alphabet games, and early reading skills.
  • Math Adventures: Counting, sorting, and shape recognition through play.
  • Creative Arts: Painting, drawing, and music to spark imagination.
  • Science Exploration: Simple experiments and nature walks to learn about the world.
  • Physical Play: Outdoor games and structured movement to develop motor skills.
